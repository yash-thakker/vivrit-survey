What Vivrit is

A child development platform built for children aged 2 to 10. It replaces passive content like YouTube Kids and Netflix cartoons with psychologist-certified stories, interactive games, and focus building activities. Every piece of content is matched to your child's age and developmental stage. Parents get a weekly dashboard showing what their child engaged with and what it is building in them. Preschools partner with Vivrit to extend their learning philosophy into the home.
